What is ROBOT LEAGUE?

The League is an exciting competition organized by the Robotics Club FSR, taking place during the summer over a span of six weeks. Teams participate in intense matches, with each team competing in five matches per week, testing their skills, strategy, and power . The fast-paced nature of the competition pushes participants to innovate and collaborate, refining their robots and tactics week by week. The League provides a unique opportunity for robotics enthusiasts to challenge themselves, while fostering a spirit of teamwork and healthy competition. With each match, teams aim to improve, adapt, and climb the ranks, culminating in a thrilling finale that showcases the best of what the league has to offer.
