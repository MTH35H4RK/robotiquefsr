Who We Are?

Pioneering Robotics Excellence Since 2008

The Robotics Club of Rabat's Faculty of Sciences is a club that brings together students who are interested in robotics, innovation, and new technology. The club's aim is to serve as a hub for individuals who wish to apply their conceptual scientifique knowledge, those who want to share or transmit their expertise, and all those who are drawn to creation. In a world where technology has become a cornerstone of our modern culture, our club employs a wide range of talents in 3D modeling, programming, electronics, and mechanics to develop increasingly unique solutions.

About Us

16 Years Of Experience
In Robotics

Founded in 2008, Robotics Club FSR champions innovation in robotics. We aim to develop beginners into experts with comprehensive training, progressing from basic robotics to advanced technology. Members compete in local and global contests, achieving notable wins, and work on advanced projects like drones and robots. We also host the prestigious CNR, reflecting our commitment to the global robotics community. Beyond a club, we are a movement propelling enthusiasts into pioneers, continuously pushing the envelope in robotics innovation.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What can I benefit from the club?

Engaging in our club helps develop essential skills in programming, electronics, and mechanics, while fostering teamwork and problem-solving. It's a pathway to a career in robotics or related fields, offering opportunities to make new friends and gain a memorable experience.

How can I become a member of your club?

To join the FSR Robotics Club, you need to be a student at the Faculty of Science in Rabat and follow the registration process at the beginning of every academic year.

Do I need any prerequisites to join?

No, you don't need any prior knowledge because once you become a member, you will go through a training program followed by a local competition to apply what you've learned.

Is the training paid?

No, the training itself is not paid; however, there is a one-time subscription fee of 100 DH to join the club.
